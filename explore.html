Here are some symptoms of common emergencies

Unresponsive Stroke

  • Facial Drooping
  • Asymmetric Limb Drooping
  • Slurred Speech
  • Loss of Balance
  • Eye Shift Changes

Anaphylactic Shock (Severe Allergic Reaction)

  • Shortness of Breath
  • Skin Changes
  • Circulation

Extreme Headache

  • Blurred Vision
  • Sensitive to Light
  • Clammy, Pale Skin
  • Fever

Heart Attack

  • Chest Pain
  • Numbness adn Tingling in arms
  • Back Pain
  • Diaphores(Sweating)
